Workflow: To make a new daily sheet, duplicate a master template into daily/ (e.g. daily/2026-04-21 Day 11.html), fill in the masthead fields, update problem text, and swap/remove scaffold blocks as needed. Print directly from the browser with Print / Save PDF.

Photo exemplar (I Do · You Do): Put your photo in uploads/, then replace the .photo-slot placeholder div with <img src="../uploads/photo.jpg">.
